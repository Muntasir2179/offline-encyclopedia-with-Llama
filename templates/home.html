Offline Encyclopedia

It is an AI powered applicaiton which enables us to upload texts data and analyze it in question answer style. It splits the texts into smaller chunks and passes through an embedidng model and stores the generated embedding into vector database. It offers to ran query on the uploaded text data. The query text is converted into an embedding and vecotr database compares the query embedding to stored embeddings and calculates distance. The lower distance indicates the most probable anser.
